THE BEST KDRAMAS ACCORDING TO ME

my top 3 dramas of all time

ITs OKAY TO NOT BE OKAY

It's about how the title say-sometimes it's okay to not be okay.
this drama has everything-comedy, romance,healing.

Hometown Cha Cha Cha

It's about a guy who held himself for his friend's burden. watch how he get rid of that as a pretty lady barges his boring silent life.
Kim Seon Ho is actually my favourite actor.

Business Proposal

a bit cringey yet fluffy, feel-good, romance-comedy kdrama which never gets boring.
